My Projects

Hotel management app

Eden hotel is a modern hotel booking app that helps travelers find and book accommodations with ease. Featuring a clean, intuitive UI, users can view immersive room images, and filter stays based on budget and preferences. With secure booking, flexible payment options, and real-time availability updates, Eden hotel app makes trip planning stress-free.

FeetnAdorn Project

FeetnAdorn is my innovative footwear project to solve key industry challenges like affordability, comfort, and accessibility. The platform features a sleek interface with intuitive navigation, high-quality visuals, and a smart search function. Users can explore product views, customize designs in real time, and enjoy a seamless checkout experience. With a responsive design for smooth performance, FeetnAdorn makes footwear shopping effortless and engaging.

Elite interior Project

Elite interior simplifies furniture shopping with an intuitive, visually rich platform. Users can browse high-quality types of furniture, customize colors and materials. The app offers easy navigation, secure checkout, and real-time delivery tracking, making furniture shopping effortless and personalized. this project was done with Phebean Adelakun.
